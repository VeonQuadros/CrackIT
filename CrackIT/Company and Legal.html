About Us

CrackIT is a premier platform designed to help IT students and professionals excel in technical interviews and aptitude tests. We offer a wide range of practice questions, mock tests, and resources covering topics like averages, profit & loss, coding challenges, and more—empowering users to crack their dream jobs with confidence.

CrackIT was built for IT students preparing for placements or seeking jobs in the IT sector. This platform helps enhance their knowledge of various aptitude topics through practice questions and mock tests, including technical and HR interview preparation.

This website was developed by students of Mahatma Gandhi Memorial Evening College as their final year project. The team consists of:
Veon Quadros, Gowrish Shet, and Sambhram Shettigar.

We are grateful to our project guide, Ms. Varshini Kotian, for her ideas, support, and guidance throughout the design and development phases.

Future Scope

CrackIT is committed to evolving with the needs of IT aspirants. We plan to add various aptitude papers from popular IT companies and provide unlimited mock tests for subscribed users. We aim to train students for technical interviews using AI and integrated code editors for coding practice. Additionally, we plan to include a feature that allows users to upload their own questions and share them with the community.
